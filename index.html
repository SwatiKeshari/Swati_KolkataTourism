The Dapper Young City

Kolkata served as the British capital till 1912, before it was shifted to Delhi. It is also the second largest city in India in area coverage after the capital city of Delhi. In fact, it was a city of significance importance to the British Empire.If you visit Kolkata, for whatever span maybe, you are bound to find out that public transport is the lifeline of the city. They are effective and fast, offering the best connectivity. From auto-rickshaws to buses, there are a wide number of options which you can avail. If you want to travel back in time, then you can definitely opt for tram, as Kolkata is one of the few cities in the entire world to have this mode of transport.
